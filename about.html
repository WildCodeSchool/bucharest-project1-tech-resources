About the Project

This website was created by two students of Wild Code School at the campus in Bucharest, Romania. At the conclusion of our first week of bootcamp, our class worked in teams to each create a website. Our team devSource had a goal to build a website that could be helpful to any person working to improve his or her programming skills.
